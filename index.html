We are AWiA.

Women farmers are faced with challenges in the agricultural market: the make up a significant portion of the agricultural labour force, constituting an average of 43 percent in developing contries; making up approximately 50 percent in Sub-Saharan Africa. However, the farmers are often faced with challenges when trying to access teh agricultural market.

Women have limited access to finance, agricultural input such as seeds, farming implements and transport. They are impacted by a lack of agricultural skills and infrastructure and the majority of them are involved in small-scaled farming due to limited resources. They additionally have limited knowledge and use of farming technologies.

AWiA would like to close this gap in knowledge and resources by empowering women. Hence, our vision is:

To capacitate women's access to the agri-ecosystem, driving socio economic impact through disruptive virtual and physical communities.
