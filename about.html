About AWiA.

African Women in Agriculture, otherwise known as AWiA, is a Non-Profit Company with a vision to capacitate women in agribusiness. We are involved in a number of projects, including the Lwazi Agripreneur Incubator, our flagship and isiVuno, a programme for small-scale and home food farmers.

We're committed to starting and growing a robust community within the emerging farmer ecosystem in Africa. That's a big statement for a small organisation, but we embrace partnerships and collaboration and welcome conversations.

Our WHY:

  • Empower women
  • Give women access
  • Create change
  • Impact hunger and poverty
  • Give back to the community
  • Make a difference
  • Create a new model
  • Drive aspiration
  • Create an ecosystem
