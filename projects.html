Projects in progess...

Women armed with tenacity

As a group of women seasoned win entrepreneurship, with a passion for agriculture, the founding cohort believed that we could make an impact in this space. The founders were determined that the focus be agri-business. Armed with tenacity and belief, AWiA was founded in 2018. In the two-plus years since our incorportaion, we've discovered that the tenacity is an absolute requirement. We've also learned that gathering the right people around this organisation is key.

Projects:

  • LWAZI - An incubator for 48 young women over three years where organic growing, hydroponics and the business of farming will be learned.
  • An organic vegetable garden on the land of a children's home where AWiA's first agripreneur was trained. Area: =/+ 1ha.
  • The inception of a virtual community of service providers for emerging farmers.
  • A cotton incubator in Limpopo in teh early stages of a business plan.
  • isiVuno - A female farmer empowerment programme aimed at changing the narraative of poverty and hunger through a 3 day course on topics such as pyramid gardening, rain harvesting, composting, and crop planning. Includes the course material, an Amaveggie Pyramid, 100 seedlings, topsoil and compost.
  • Grow 4 Good - A programme that allows for participants to help the planting and growth of trees at the AWiA farm in Eikenhof, South of Johannesburg, to help combat CO2 emissions. Partnered with EcoForce.
